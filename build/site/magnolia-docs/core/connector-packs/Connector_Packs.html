Connector Packs

Magnolia offers the following Connector Packs tailored to your market goals:

  • Analytics Connector Pack – Display key analytics metrics from connected systems in Magnolia.

  • Commerce Connector Pack – Combine content and commerce. Search through your product catalogs and embed product data.

  • Optimization Connector Pack – Choose the best-performing content for your site. Check for errors and missing metadata.

  • DAM Connector Pack – Manage large numbers of assets, large assets. Handle specialized image needs such as tagging.

  • Marketing Automation Connector Pack – Capture leads and connect to existing customer data for personalization.

  • Optimization Connector Pack – Choose the best performing content for your site using A/B testing and connectors like Siteimprove.

Each Connector Pack is composed of a number of carefully selected modules that serve specific requirements.
