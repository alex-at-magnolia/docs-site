Stay updated

There are many ways to stay up-to-date with Magnolia. Choose the options that best suit your work style and requirements.

Official documentation

We update the documentation site continually and you can set preferences in your personal space for update notifications. Use your JIRA credentials to log in and go to <Your Name> > Settings > Email to set your preferences. Register a JIRA account if you don’t have one. There are numerous email options and you can also Favorite other users and Watch pages for updates.

On each documentation page, you can Like and Comment. Feel free to comment and show your support. We welcome your feedback. Pages are monitored continuously and a staff or community member will answer promptly. Asking technical questions on our forums is often a better alternative to commenting because your issue is exposed to a broader base - Magnolia staff and highly-skilled community members, who are always happy to lend a hand.

Forum and mailing lists

The forum and mailing lists are fundamental parts of the Magnolia community. Magnolia Forums and Mailing lists are now available as Google Groups.

Please subscribe to our lists or visit to see and ask new questions. Post your questions and they’ll be answered by members of the Magnolia community and staff.

Announcements and News

https://groups.google.com/a/magnolia-cms.com/forum/#!forum/announce-list

Users and General Questions
For Magnolia users of all technical levels. Find help, help others and share your experiences.

https://groups.google.com/a/magnolia-cms.com/forum/#!forum/user-list

Developers
For developers to exchange information and keep up-to-date on development status. Notifications from the JIRA issue tracker are sent here which makes this a very busy list.

https://groups.google.com/a/magnolia-cms.com/forum/#!forum/dev-list

Newsletter

Keep up to date with all things Magnolia by signing up for one of our monthly newsletters. You can also opt-in for updates on events, resources and product updates.

Blogs

Find insider tips and tricks at the Magnolia Blog.

Community wiki

The Community wiki is where Magnolia developers and experienced partners and clients share their knowledge. There’s are "How-tos", tutorials and more. You can participate and share your knowledge with others by creating useful pages.

Developer wiki

The Developer wiki is where our developers document new concepts, ideas and projects.

Social

Follow Magnolia on the best social networks for the latest news and periodic events. Our YouTube channel has tutorials, webinar recordings and more.
